@justhum

Hum started as a side-project between Aaron Shekey and Joseph Kuefler. They were both tired of every song they wrote ending up captured as a voice memo with only the most basic meta data attached to it. They didn’t want their lyrics ending up as fragments between shopping lists and reminders. In the spring of 2013, Aaron quit his job at Adobe to pursue the working on the initial designs of Hum. Joseph spent the next few weeks on the branding.

Aaron Shekey

Aaron cut his teeth making websites in Minneapolis with Sevnthsin. He then founded and designed Edge Reflow while at Adobe. He leads the Minneapolis-based band The Usual Things. He designed Hum.

@aaronshekey

Joseph Kuefler

Joseph came up as a designer. Now, he leads creative teams at Zeus Jones where he’s worked with the likes of Google, Purina, Nordstrom, and Cheerios. He’s a songwriter and multi-instrumentalist. He branded Hum.

@josephkuefler
